Welcome to the Meet with a AAAI Fellow Program

Thursday - Saturday, February 4-7

First held in 2006, the AAAI Fellow/Student program provides an opportunity for a small number of students to chat with a AAAI Fellow in an informal setting during the conference. This year the program will be held via Virtual Chair on Gather.Town. Participation will be on a first-come, first-served basis in Room E of Virtual Chair on Gather.town. A volunteer will assist you in locating your chosen Fellow at the designated time.

Fellows will generally be available for one hour in one of the following time periods. A full schedule is included below.
