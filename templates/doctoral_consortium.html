Welcome to the Twenty-Sixth AAAI/SIGAI Doctoral Consortium

February 2-3, 2021

Collocated with the Thirty-Fifth Conference on Artificial Intelligence (AAAI-21)

{#

External site: https://aaaidc.github.io/dc2021/

#}

The Twenty-Sixth AAAI/SIGAI Doctoral Consortium (DC) provides an opportunity for a group of Ph.D. students to discuss and explore their research interests and career objectives in an interdisciplinary workshop together with a panel of established researchers. The eighteen students accepted to participate in this program will also participate in the AAAI-21 Poster Sessions on Thursday, February 4. All interested AAAI-21 student registrants are invited to observe the presentations.

The full Doctoral Consortium schedule will be included below.

AAAI and SIGAI gratefully acknowledge the generous grant from the National Science Foundation, which makes this program possible.

Shiwali Mohan and Anna Reali

Doctoral Consortium Cochairs
